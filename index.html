Sequence of Operations (SOO)

▾

1. Fan & Ventilation Control

  • Unit Enable: Supply fan starts when toggled. Return fan is sized at 35% of supply fan bank capacity and starts if configured.
  • VFD Control: Speed modulates to maintain setpoint. Supply fan VFD has a 120-second ramp speed; return fan VFD has a 90-second ramp speed.
  • Starter Control: Fan runs at 100% capacity; dampers modulate air flow.
  • Ventilation / Economizer: Dampers modulate to maintain mixed-air temp setpoint (economizer active when OAT is favorable).

2. Temperature Control

  • Preheat Coil: Modulates to keep coil leaving air temp above 38°F.
  • Cooling Coil: Modulates to maintain supply/cold-deck SAT at setpoint (typically 55°F).
  • Reheat Coil (Single-Duct): Modulates to maintain supply air temperature setpoint.
  • Hot Deck Coil (Dual-Duct): Modulates to maintain hot-deck temperature setpoint.

3. Humidity & Room Reset

  • Humidification: Modulates steam valve to track Return RH against low limit setpoint. Clamps single-duct supply RH to ≤85%.
  • Dehumidification: Triggers if Return RH exceeds high limit, or if OR/PR space RH exceeds 50%. Limits SAT/Cold Deck to ≤60°F, clamps box reheats to ≤Zone SP + 5°F, and locks hot deck OA.
  • OR/PR Reset: Space RH >50% resets zone temp SP up by up to 3°F to drop local relative humidity.

4. Safety & Lockout Sequences

  • Freezestat: Shuts down fans, forces heating valves 100% open, and latches if coil temp falls below 38°F.
  • High Static: Trips and latches if duct pressure exceeds 2.5" w.g., shutting down VFDs.
  • Aquastat: Trips preheat booster pumps on water flow failure to prevent freeze-ups.
